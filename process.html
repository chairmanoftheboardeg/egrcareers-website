Hiring Process

What to expect after you apply

Our recruitment process is designed to protect standards and ensure every staff member can operate professionally.

1) Application submission

Apply for a specific role. Ensure your Discord ID is correct to avoid onboarding delays.

2) HR review

HR reviews applications based on clarity, professionalism, consistency, and alignment with role standards.

3) Decision

Accepted or rejected. Some roles may require follow-up questions before acceptance.

4) Discord onboarding

If accepted, your staff role is assigned automatically and you receive the onboarding pack via Discord DM.

What we evaluate

ProfessionalismHow you communicate and present intent.
AccountabilityAbility to follow standards and execute reliably.
Role fitAlignment with responsibilities and expectations.
