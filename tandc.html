Employee Terms & Conditions

The Emirates Group Roblox HR Department enforces rules and guidelines to maintain a safe, professional, and realistic work environment that reflects the real-world Emirates corporate experience.

1. Code of Conduct

  • Employees must behave professionally at all times.
  • Harassment, discrimination, or misconduct is strictly prohibited.
  • Employees must follow rank hierarchy and respect HR leadership.

2. Attendance & Activity

  • Staff activity checks may occur weekly.
  • Multiple inactivity periods without notice may result in removal.
  • Leave of absence must be submitted formally to HR.

3. Confidentiality

  • Internal documents, discussions, and processes must not be leaked.
  • HR decisions must remain internal.
  • Violations may result in suspension or blacklisting.

4. Termination of Employment

Emirates Group Roblox reserves the right to terminate employment for misconduct, inactivity, breach of confidentiality, or failure to follow HR instructions.

5. Agreement

By joining Emirates Group Roblox, you agree to comply with all policies listed in these Terms & Conditions.
