Working with Emirates Group Roblox (EGR)

At EGR, we believe that aviation excellence begins with people. Every member of our organisation—no matter the department or rank—is valued, respected, and given a chance to grow through structured training, mentorship, and leadership opportunities. Our mission is to create a community that mirrors the professionalism of the real aviation industry, while empowering every individual to reach their highest potential.

A Message from Our Chief Human Resources Officer (CHRO)

“At Emirates Group Roblox, we believe people are at the heart of everything we do. Human Resources isn’t just about management—it’s about empowerment. Every flight, every operation, and every success begins with someone who believed they could make a difference. My role as CHRO is to ensure that every team member, regardless of position, feels valued, guided, and inspired to grow. Together, we uphold the professionalism, teamwork, and respect that make EGR a truly extraordinary organisation.”

— Sarah Al Qasimi, Chief Human Resources Officer

Why Work with EGR?

  • Realistic Role-Play with Professional Standards: We go beyond simulation to create a true reflection of aviation operations—professionalism, structure, and precision are at the core of what we do.
  • Comprehensive Training and Mentorship: Every recruit undergoes detailed onboarding, learning directly from senior mentors who guide them through EGR’s standards, culture, and procedures.
  • Clear Career Progression and Leadership: With effort, commitment, and teamwork, you can rise through the ranks—from Trainee to Executive or Department Head—based purely on performance and dedication.
  • Respect, Integrity, and Teamwork: Our members are our family. We nurture trust, provide feedback, and encourage open communication at every level of the organisation.

Our Commitment to Staff Development

At EGR, learning never stops. We provide our members with access to aviation-inspired workshops, soft-skills development, and leadership training. We encourage every individual to step out of their comfort zone and take initiative.

Respect and professionalism guide every decision we make. When you join EGR, you become part of a united, goal-driven community that values your voice and recognises your contribution.

Our Initiative

The EGR Careers Pathway Initiative offers members real-life experience in teamwork, customer service, and aviation operations. We host community training, mock flight operations, and mentorship sessions designed to build skills applicable in both virtual and real aviation careers.

Our purpose is simple: to help passionate individuals find their wings, grow as professionals, and become future leaders of the aviation world—both in Roblox and beyond.
