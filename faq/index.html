Frequently Asked Questions

Find detailed answers regarding our recruitment policies, requirements, and the application review process.

Our Recruitment Officers operate strictly within standard HR hours. Applications are typically reviewed within 48 to 72 hours. During high-volume periods or specialized hiring cycles, reviews may take up to 7 days. You can track your real-time status using our Status Portal.
To maintain operational excellence, Flight Operations (Pilots) must use a PC or Desktop. Mobile or Tablet devices are strictly prohibited for flight deck controls. However, Cabin Crew and Ground Operations may apply using mobile devices, provided they can uphold our strict roleplay and communication standards.
