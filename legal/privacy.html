Privacy Policy
This policy explains what information we collect through the Careers website and how it’s used.

1. What we collect

When you apply, we collect information you provide such as name, email, Discord username/ID, optional Roblox username, and your answers to application questions.

2. How we use it

We use this information to process applications, contact candidates, and manage internal recruitment operations.

3. Who can access it

Only authorised HR staff can view and manage applications through the HR dashboard.

4. Links

If you provide a CV/resume link, you are responsible for what is shared at that link. We recommend using privacy-safe links and only sharing what is necessary for recruitment.

5. Retention

Applications may be retained for recruitment records and future opportunities, unless removed by HR.

6. Security

We use access controls (role-based permissions) to limit HR dashboard access. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

7. Contact

If you have privacy concerns, contact EGR via official HR channels.
