Compliance Framework
High standards keep the organisation professional. This page summarises the expectations for staff conduct and compliance.

1. Professional conduct

Staff are expected to communicate respectfully, follow leadership direction, and maintain a professional culture.

2. Operational integrity

Departments follow SOPs and training standards. Repeated failure to comply may lead to corrective action.

3. Safety and reporting

Safety concerns and serious operational issues should be reported via appropriate internal channels.

4. HR and discipline

HR may investigate misconduct and apply internal disciplinary processes as defined by EGR’s governance structures.

5. Platform compliance

All members must comply with Roblox rules and any community rules communicated through official EGR channels.

This page is a summary. Your full internal Legal, Safety & Compliance documentation can be linked here later.
