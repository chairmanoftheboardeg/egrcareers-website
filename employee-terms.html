Employee Terms & Conditions — Emirates Group Roblox

Effective date:
Legal notice: Emirates Group Roblox is an independent virtual organisation and is not affiliated with any real airline. This document is an internal policy and does not create an employment contract. For legal disputes contact a licensed attorney.

1. Scope & Purpose

This document outlines the rights, obligations and policies applicable to all personnel who engage with Emirates Group Roblox in any capacity, including volunteers, contractors, moderators and staff.

2. Recruitment & Eligibility Standards

Applicants must provide truthful information, confirm identity and meet minimum eligibility requirements. Underage policies apply: those under 13 cannot participate in staff roles and use of the service by minors may be restricted.

3. Data handling & Privacy (employees)

Personal data submitted during recruitment and employment is stored for administrative and compliance purposes. Access to personal data is strictly limited to HR staff and authorised officers. Requests for deletion or copies of personal data should be submitted via the Support Centre.

4. Confidentiality & Sensitive Information

Employees will at times receive operational or sensitive information. Such information must not be shared externally. Breach of confidentiality is a serious disciplinary matter and may result in immediate removal.

5. Code of Conduct

Staff must act professionally, treat members respectfully and follow SOPs. Harassment, discrimination or illegal behaviour will be subject to investigation and sanction.

6. Grievance & Dispute Resolution

Any complaints should be directed to HR. The Group will investigate impartially and will provide a reasoned outcome where practicable.

7. Termination & Appeal

Where termination action is taken, the staff member has the right to appeal via the HR appeals process as described in the Support Centre documentation.

8. Liability

The Group operates as a virtual community. It disclaims liability to the maximum extent permitted by law for indirect losses, except where liability cannot be excluded by law.

9. Amendments

The Group may update these terms. Material changes will be communicated. Continued participation after posting shall indicate acceptance.
