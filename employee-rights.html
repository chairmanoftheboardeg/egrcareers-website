Employee Rights & Terms — Emirates Group Roblox

Comprehensive terms for staff and applicants (US remote focus).

Effective date:

1. Purpose & Scope

This Employee Rights document sets out the terms, rights and obligations for anyone engaged by Emirates Group Roblox ("the Group"). It covers volunteers, contractors and paid staff working remotely in the United States and is intended to ensure transparent, fair and lawful treatment.

2. Employment Status & Classification

Staff are engaged under one of the following classifications: volunteer, contractor, or employee. Classification affects eligibility for compensation, benefits, and legal protections. The classification will always be confirmed in a written engagement letter or contract.

3. Equal Opportunity & Anti-Discrimination

The Group provides equal opportunity in recruitment, employment, and all personnel actions. Discrimination or harassment based on protected characteristics is strictly prohibited. Complaints are investigated promptly and impartially.

4. Recruitment & Selection

Vacancies are advertised on the careers site and official channels. Applicants are selected based on transparent criteria and job-relevant competencies. HR will provide reasonable feedback and an application status on request.

5. Remote Work & Availability

Remote staff must agree working hours with managers. HR office hours (Dubai time) are published on the careers portal. Reasonable adjustments are considered; staff must communicate planned absences in advance.

6. Pay, Expense & Payment Terms

Contracted roles include payment schedules in the contract. Volunteers are not automatically paid. Expenses will only be reimbursed where pre-approved and supported by receipts or valid proof.

7. Confidentiality, Data Protection & Privacy

Employees must not disclose confidential Group data, user information, or private communications. The Group stores personal data required for HR functions; requests to access or delete personal data should be submitted to Support. The Group will make reasonable efforts to comply with applicable privacy laws.

8. Code of Conduct & Professional Standards

Staff must behave professionally in public and private representations of the Group. Harassment, abusive language, doxxing, impersonation or illegal activities are strictly forbidden and may result in immediate suspension or termination.

9. Intellectual Property

Materials created in the course of duties for the Group (guides, images, code, assets) are assigned to the Group unless a separate written agreement specifies otherwise.

10. Grievance & Disciplinary Procedures

Concerns should be raised to HR via the Support Centre. The Group will investigate and provide the opportunity to respond. Disciplinary measures are proportionate to the misconduct and may include warnings, suspension, or removal.

11. Termination

Where roles are paid or contracted, termination terms will be in the contract. For volunteers, termination may occur for cause or by mutual agreement. The Group will seek to act transparently and will provide reasons where possible.

12. Health, Safety & Online Security

Staff should follow good online security practices: strong passwords, avoid sharing credentials, and report suspicious activity immediately. If an online safety incident occurs, report to HR and platform moderators.

13. Dispute Resolution

Where disputes cannot be resolved through HR, the parties may request an impartial review. For legal matters, parties may seek external mediation or legal counsel.

14. Amendments

The Group may update these terms. Material changes will be communicated through official channels. Continued engagement implies acceptance of updated terms.

This document is intended as a comprehensive internal policy for Emirates Group Roblox and is not a substitute for formal legal contracts or professional legal advice. For binding contracts or jurisdiction-specific obligations consult a qualified attorney.
