Corporate Structure

Our Departments

Flight Operations

Responsible for the safe and professional execution of all flights. Includes Flight Deck, Cabin Services, and Ground Operations.

Operations Control Center

The central nervous system monitoring live server capacity, dispatching flights, and coordinating ATC.

EGR Aviation University

Dedicated to standardizing training and examination for all operational staff prior to live server deployment.

Human Resources

Manages recruitment, staff well-being, internal promotions, and the active roster.

Technology & Development

The engineering backbone responsible for Roblox studio building, scripting, and web systems.

Commercial Affairs

Oversees public relations, strategic alliances, marketing campaigns, and passenger experience.
